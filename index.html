About

BEST education institution in Simara

Welcome to ST.ALPHONSA'S SCHOOL

St. Alphonsa's school, Simara, Bara District, Nepal, is established and administered by the Nepal Adoration Society (Reg. No. 903/054/55). The Sisters of the Adoration of the Blessd Sacrament (SABS) is managing the different activities of the society and St. Alphonsa's School is a part of its education apostolate. It is an English medium and coducational institution.
